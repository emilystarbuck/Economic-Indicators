Washington County Economic Indicators


July 2017
Washington County's unemployment rate was 3.3 percent in July 2017, a 1.1 percentage point drop from the prior year. Many cities in Washington County also saw unemployment rates lower than the previous year (not seasonally adjusted).

The county added 6,600 jobs between July 2016 and July 2017. Transportation and warehousing continued to increase with a 13.6 percent growth rate. No industries declined in employment over the year.
